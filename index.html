About the Trip Planner

This trip planning website is a comprehensive tool designed to streamline the travel experience from inspiration to execution. It offers a dual approach: providing expertly suggested trips based on specific travel styles and offering a robust interface for users to build their own detailed itineraries.

By integrating logistical details with creative personalization, the platform ensures every aspect of a journey—from budget and activities to daily outfits and energy levels—is meticulously organized in one place.

Core Features

  • Intelligent Suggestions: Browse curated trips categorized by energy level and theme.
  • Custom Itinerary Builder: Detailed input for locations, activities, and time-specific plans.
  • Comprehensive Tracking: Manage budgets, food preferences, and even outfit suggestions for each day.
  • Interactive Mapping: Integrated maps with flagged restaurants, sights, and activity locations.
  • Trip Synchronization: Create a user account to save, modify, and revisit your plans at any time.
